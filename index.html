Janice Murphy

A tribute to my lovely grandmother

Here is Janice's Impact on All of Us

  • She would always share her many wonderful pies including peach and apple.
  • Her skill at card games such as BS and Gin Rummy were incredible. She had the best poker face with a hint of a smile
  • She had so many strong thoughts on politics and democracy, her passion shown through when we talked over dinner
  • She had a thirst for travel that I envy, using her passion to travel the world with us and others.
  • She had the nicest hugs whenever we visited. You could tell that she really cared about everyone around her
  • Despite having the struggles that she did living through the Great Depression and having difficult relationships, she always came out stronger
